Generation AI at ITK2025 Event

25.04.2025

Generation AI Project Greetings from ITK2025

The Generation AI project was strongly represented at the 2025 Interactive Technology in Education (ITK) event, which is the largest educational technology event in our country.

ITK Forum

Why is social media addictive? How does artificial intelligence work?

Artificial intelligence is like a black box, its contents equally a mystery to children, young people and teachers alike. It is clear that we need tools, methods and content to help children and young people learn about this phenomenon.

At the ITK Forum, Megumi Iwata presented a new prototype interface for the social media machine that addresses the effects of social media, and an early version of educational materials designed for it.

From the accompanying materials, you can explore these prototype stage contents and interface!

ITK Arena

Generation AI 3 years: What is the foundation of AI and safety education? What's next?

In this Arena presentation, we addressed our three-year journey as developers of AI and safety education through brief expert statements. We provided participants with balanced perspectives on the topic from pedagogical, technological and legal viewpoints. After the presentations, a panel discussion followed where the themes of the research presentations were further explored.

Privacy policy

General information

The use of the educational materials on the site does not require the disclosure of personal data. The educational materials are divided into materials published on this website (the Privacy Policy of this website) and applications outside this website (the applications' own privacy policies).

It should also be noted that the materials are published under the Creative Commons BY-SA license and the applications under the MIT license. There is nothing to stop you from using educational materials or apps in your teaching.

Generation AI project in the media

What on earth is artificial intelligence? What's it all about?

What on earth is artificial intelligence? Prof. Teemu Roos explains

Our project staff are also known from the media. On this page you'll find many interviews and other accessible content that help you understand this complex phenomenon called artificial intelligence! Prof. Teemu Roos, who works in our project, has distinguished himself by popularizing

Artificial intelligence is not science fiction, but it's already present in our everyday lives, says the Professor of Computer Science at the University of Helsinki. He leads AI teaching at Finland's AI center FCAI and is the responsible teacher for the popular Elements of AI online course.

Prof. Teemu Roos in KU magazine interview 27.8.2023. https://www.ku.fi/artikkeli/4906774-tekoaly-on-jo-taalla-eika-siihen-pida-suhtautua-kuin-luonnonvoimaan-sanoo-tietojenkasittelytieteen-professori-teemu-roos

Heureka has published the Skilled AI learning package, which introduces machine learning, AI ethics, and supercomputing themes for school teaching.

24.01.2026

New tools and materials integration site!

New tools and materials integration site is now open! Welcome to explore! Community, feedback and sharing tools will be added to the site later, but even as it is, this update is a major step forward. Materials are no longer on the project website, but together with our unique AI tools designed for educational use, they form a comprehensive whole. The site can be found here: https://www.gen-ai.fi

24.12.2025

Generation AI at EDUCA2025 event

Welcome to the EDUCA2026 event to try out our new applications, especially the small language engine. The small language engine is the world's first language model that works in a browser (no separate server needed) and can be trained with your own data. At our booth, you can also take a look at our new website (https://www.gen-ai.fi), which brings together our applications and teaching materials into one complete package! Welcome to booth 6k18 at the convention center!

27.05.2025

The Generation AI project promoting AI literacy in children and young people has won the 2025 national Open Educational Resource Award.

The Generation AI project promoting AI literacy in children and young people has won the 2025 national Open Educational Resource Award. The award was presented on Tuesday, May 27, at the Open Science Summer Days in Helsinki. According to the award presenters, the Generation AI project is highly timely, the materials are openly available, and they are based on co-design, which is why the award for open educational material of the year was decided to be given to this project. 28.04.2025

GenAI Teachable Machine is included in Microsoft's FarmBeats for Students package

Generation AI's Teachable Machine is now used as part of Microsoft's FarmBeats for Students STEM package. The Microsoft Learn module is a beginner-level 10-unit module for K-12 educators.

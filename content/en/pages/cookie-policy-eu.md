## What this page covers

This page explains how the Generation AI website uses cookies and other browser-side storage.

## What the site stores

The site uses the following necessary storage mechanisms:

- `cc_cookie` to store your cookie preferences
- browser `localStorage` to store accessibility settings under the key `genai_a11y`

These are used so that the site functions correctly and your chosen settings persist.

## Consent-based services

The site may also use third-party services that are loaded only when needed or when you have given consent.

These include:

- `MailerLite` for newsletter management
- `YouTube-nocookie` when you start an embedded video

Marketing-related scripts are not loaded before consent.

## Analytics

The site uses `Cloudflare Web Analytics` for aggregate website analytics.

The on-site search uses `Pagefind`, which runs locally within the site implementation.

## What we do not use

The site does not use Google Analytics or Google Tag Manager.

## Managing your settings

You can update your cookie preferences through the cookie banner. You can also remove previously stored cookies and local storage entries in your browser settings.

If you remove necessary storage, some site functions or preferences may return to their default state.

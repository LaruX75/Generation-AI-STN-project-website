<p>Generation AI held the project's first planning meeting at Heureka, which is a member of the project consortium. The location was indeed perfect for the meeting, as Heureka opened a new artificial intelligence-themed exhibition on 22.6.2022.</p>



<p>After the planning meeting, consortium members familiarized themselves with Heureka's Artificial Intelligence exhibition and other Heureka activities related to artificial intelligence, robotics, and programming.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">What is the Artificial Intelligence Exhibition About?</h4>



<p>On Heureka's website, the exhibition is described as follows</p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p>The Artificial Intelligence – Me, Myself &amp; AI exhibition addresses the fundamentals of artificial intelligence development, the interaction between humans and robots guided by artificial intelligence, and the social dimensions of artificial intelligence development in a fun and interactive way.</p>



<p>Artificial intelligence is already part of our everyday lives in many ways. Since we humans build artificial intelligence, it is also good for us to think together about what kind of artificial intelligence we want and how we want to use it.</p>



<h5 class="wp-block-heading">Welcome to the World of Artificial Intelligence!</h5>



<p>The Artificial Intelligence exhibition features a story about a Scientist working in the field of artificial intelligence. In the Artificial Intelligence exhibition, you can visit the Scientist's home, garden, and workshop. The Scientist has developed fun artificial intelligence-powered devices for his home. He is interested in researching what we can do together with artificial intelligence.</p>

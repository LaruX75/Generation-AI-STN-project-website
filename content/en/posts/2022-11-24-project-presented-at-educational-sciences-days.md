<p>The Generation AI project participated in the 2022 Educational Sciences Days on 24-25.11.2022. </p>



<p>The Educational Sciences Association organizes Educational Sciences Days annually in collaboration with a Finnish university. Educational Sciences Days is the most significant domestic science event in the field of education. Over 600 educational scientists visited the days held in Oulu.</p>



<p>The Generation AI project employs educational scientists from the Universities of Helsinki, Eastern Finland, and Oulu. Professor Kati Mäkitalo, responsible for the project's interaction, was the head of the planning group for the Oulu Educational Sciences Days.</p>



<p>The project, in its launch phase, presented the project's activities through a poster presentation. A poster is an A0-sized poster used to present scientific research or other topics to conference participants.</p>

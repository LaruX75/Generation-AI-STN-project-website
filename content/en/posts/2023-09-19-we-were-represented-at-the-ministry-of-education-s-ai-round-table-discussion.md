<p>The Generation AI project participated in a round table discussion organized by the Ministry of Education on 19.9.2023. The event brought together a comprehensive group of Finnish researchers, developers and teachers from different levels of education. <br><br>Before the round table, Jari Laru, representing the Generation AI project, discussed cooperation with representatives of the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Education and presented the objectives of the Generation AI project.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">More information about the AI round table discussion:</h2>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p>Artificial intelligence is currently the subject of active discussion and deliberation. The possibilities of AI applications and the ethical and legal issues related to their use are essential topics when we consider the development and future of education. The Ministry of Education is organizing a round table discussion on the use of artificial intelligence. We approach the subject especially from the perspective of basic education, upper secondary education and vocational education in a situation where, among other things, population development challenges the provision of education on an equal basis in the coming years and data on youth well-being is concerning.&nbsp;</p>
<cite>The Ministry of Education's description text in the invitation letter for the discussion.</cite></blockquote>

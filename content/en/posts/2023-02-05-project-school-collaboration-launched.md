<p>The GenAI project's school collaboration launched on Friday, February 3, 2023 from 9-14 at a joint planning meeting in Joensuu. The event included 7 primary schools, 3 lower secondary schools, and 2 comprehensive schools from the Joensuu area.</p>



<p>During the workshop, GenAI project director Professor Matti Tedre introduced the basic concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning, as well as the transformation in data-driven thinking. After the lecture, participants had the opportunity to practically test various cutting-edge technologies with the support of computer scientists.</p>



<p>Since school interventions are planned through collaboration between teachers and researchers, the day also included outlining the project's implementation and the spring 2023 schedule.<br><br>School collaboration is coordinated by Joensuu Media Center, which both supports the city's schools in various technology-related matters and organizes and coordinates collaborative projects between the University of Eastern Finland and schools. According to Mari Levy, representative of the Media Center, the upcoming six-year joint journey will be amazing!</p>

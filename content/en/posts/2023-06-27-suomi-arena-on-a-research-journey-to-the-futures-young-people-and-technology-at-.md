You can watch the panel recording here:<br><a href="https://www.mtv.fi/sarja/suomiareena-1464/tutkimusmatkalla-tulevaisuuksiin-ohjaimisssa-nuoret-ja-teknologia-20594602">https://www.mtv.fi/sarja/suomiareena-1464/tutkimusmatkalla-tulevaisuuksiin-ohjaimisssa-nuoret-ja-teknologia-20594602</a></em></p>



<p>Join young people on a research journey into the future to find answers to how schooling, learning, and technology are developing: What and how do we learn? What role does technology play in our daily lives? The School of the Future is participating in SuomiArena – come and follow the discussion!<a href="https://www.mtv.fi/sarja/suomiareena-1464/tutkimusmatkalla-tulevaisuuksiin-ohjaimisssa-nuoret-ja-teknologia-20594602" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"></a></p>



<p><strong>What</strong>: Panel discussion On a research journey to the futures – young people and technology at the helm<br><strong>When</strong>: June 27, 10:00–10:45<br><strong>Where</strong>: SuomiArena, Raatihuoneenpuisto stage (Teatterikatu 7, Pori) or MTV Katsomo.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Panelists</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Minna Huotilainen,&nbsp;</strong>brain researcher, professor, University of Helsinki</li>



<li><strong>Priska Niemi-Sampan</strong>, social psychologist (M.Phil.), non-fiction author, Mixed Finns ry</li>

<li><strong>Otto Tähkäpää,&nbsp;</strong>leading expert at the School of the Future</li>

<p>The research journey will be guided by Porkkaala Upper Secondary School students&nbsp;<strong>Ellen Tolkki</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>Milja Pulli</strong>. Welcome to follow the discussion, which explores what kind of future we want to pursue.</p>
